Our Founder, Jane Doe

A Harvard grad from humble beginnings, Jane started her career as an engineer for the powerhouse consulting firm Consulting & Consultants. She had long been dissatisfied with the lack of diversity throughout her educational journey and corporate line of work, but wasn't sure what she could do about it.

Meanwhile during the pandemic, she developed a passion for baking and started to sell her creations as a hobby. With the business quickly expanding and Jane still working out of her kitchen, her mother and grandmother were roped into helping her run the business. With their support, Jane decided to leave her full-time job and pursue her new passion, dedicating her proceeds to helping underrepresented groups in STEM.

Today, Jane is still heavily involved in the company's foundation and philanthrophic efforts.
